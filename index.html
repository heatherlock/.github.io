Hi, I'm Heather Lockwood, a Software Developer with a passion for Machine Learning. Three years ago, I co-founded a startup with the intent of helping people move better by providing automated movement feedback from video input. With my background as a personal trainer and strength coach, I served as the domain expert for our team, where I guided the data collection effort among other things. As something of a jill-of-all-trades, I built custom Deep Learning workstations, helped develop a video annotation tool that enabled efficiently labeling 1200+ videos per hour, and co-designed our award-winning pitch deck. With this experience, I began to discover some of the wonderful things that can be done with data. Completely dazzled by the fact that I could give a computer a set of examples and let it learn from them instead of having to tell it what it should know, I decided to pursue a formal education in computer science. I currently work as a Program Coordinator at the University of California - San Diego where I oversee a Post-Rehabilitation Program (called WokStrong) for UCSD Health employees injured on the job. A Program Coordinator doesn't sound like a role that would traditionally involve any software development, however, I’ve developed a web application to help our staff and students monitor their behavior change, developed BI dashboards, performed statistical tests on program data, and created scripts to automate business workflow eliminating hours of manual labor on a weekly basis!
